Sources for Data

**Baumsnow**
All data files retrieved from paper “Did Highways Cause Suburbanization?“ at Nate Baumsnow’s website at https://sites.google.com/site/baumsnow/research

**CBD Codes***
CBD Codes - Matt Holian, Google Earth, http://mattholian.blogspot.com/2013/05/central-business-district-geocodes.html

**Crosswalks**
State fips crosswalk - Census,FIPS Codes for the States and the District of Columbia, https://www.census.gov/geo/reference/ansi_statetables.html

SMA Crosswalk 1950 - Census, STANDARD METROPOLITAN AREAS (SMAs) AND COMPONENTS, 1950, WITH FIPS CODES, https://www.census.gov/population/estimates/metro-city/50mfips.txt

**Lee and Lin data**
All data files retrieved from supplemental data section of paper “Natural Amenities, Neighbourhood Dynamics, and Persistence in the Spatial Distribution of Income” by Sanghoon Lee  Jeffrey Lin at ReStud link https://academic.oup.com/restud/advance-article/doi/10.1093/restud/rdx018/3091867 

**NHGIS**
All data files retrieved from tables at https://www.nhgis.org/

**NLSY**
All data files retrieved from http://www.nlsinfo.org/web-investigator/. Geocode files (not included) only given by application. See https://www.bls.gov/nls/nlsfaqs.htm#anch25

**UCR data**
Uniform crime reports data from ICPSR at http://www.icpsr.umich.edu/icpsrweb/NACJD/studies/3666 
and from archive.org at https://archive.org/details/uniformcrimerepo1950unit
